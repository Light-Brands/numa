# Malta Loan Repayment — Action Plan

> 2-week execution timeline. All steps should be coordinated with Mexican tax counsel.

## Immediate (Days 1–3)

### Documentation Gathering
- [ ] Locate the original convertible note agreement with the Costa Rica company
- [ ] Obtain any assignment or novation documents showing transfer of obligation from Costa Rica to Malta
- [ ] Request historical bank statements from the closed personal account (contact the bank — records are retained for years even after closure)
- [ ] Request historical records from the inactive S.A. de C.V. (check Registro Público de Comercio, contact former accountant)
- [ ] Request formal confirmation letter from Malta company stating:
  - The nature of the payment (loan repayment / return of principal)
  - The amount ($350,000)
  - Reference to the original note
  - Confirmation that no interest is included (if applicable)

### Professional Engagement
- [ ] Engage a Mexican tax attorney (abogado fiscalista) — share this entire document set
- [ ] Engage a Mexican CPA if not already retained
- [ ] Determine if Maltese legal confirmation is needed

### Banking Setup
- [ ] Identify or open a USD-capable personal bank account at a major Mexican bank
- [ ] Notify the bank's compliance department about the incoming international transfer
- [ ] Provide the bank with supporting documentation

## Week 1 (Days 4–7)

### Legal Review
- [ ] Tax attorney reviews all documentation and confirms classification strategy
- [ ] Tax attorney advises on any required pre-filing with SAT
- [ ] Confirm whether Form 76 or other informative declaration is required
- [ ] Tax attorney reviews Mexico-Malta tax treaty applicability

### Wire Preparation
- [ ] Send receiving bank's wire instructions to Malta company
- [ ] Confirm wire reference will include loan repayment language
- [ ] Confirm expected transfer date

## Week 2 (Days 8–14)

### Fund Receipt
- [ ] Receive $350,000 wire from Malta
- [ ] Obtain bank transaction confirmation
- [ ] Document exchange rate if funds are converted to MXN
- [ ] Verify the wire reference matches documentation

### Compliance Filing
- [ ] File any required informative declarations (Form 76 if applicable)
- [ ] Prepare documentation package for SAT audit file
- [ ] Tax attorney confirms all compliance steps are complete

## Post-Receipt (Within 30 Days)

- [ ] Organize complete documentation file:
  - Original convertible note
  - Assignment/novation documents
  - Historical bank records (outflow)
  - Malta confirmation letter
  - Bank receipt (inflow)
  - Tax attorney opinion letter
- [ ] Plan allocation/distribution of funds per the recipient's intentions
- [ ] Prepare for inclusion in annual tax declaration (Declaración Anual)
- [ ] Retain all records for minimum 5 years

## Key Decision Points

| Decision | Who Decides | By When |
|----------|------------|---------|
| Classification (principal vs. interest vs. gain) | Tax attorney + taxpayer | Day 3 |
| Bank account selection | Taxpayer | Day 3 |
| Whether to file preventive SAT consultation | Tax attorney | Day 5 |
| Wire transfer date | Malta company + taxpayer | Day 7 |
| Fund allocation strategy | Taxpayer | Post-receipt |
