## Key Cost Categories

1. **Land Acquisition**: $5,500,000 (partially equity)
2. **Infrastructure & Utilities**: Roads, water, sewer, electrical, telecommunications
3. **Engineering & Technical Studies**: Topographic, geotechnical, hydrological, civil
4. **Permitting & Compliance**: Environmental, municipal, condominium regime
5. **Architectural Design**: Master plan, residential designs, guidelines
6. **Construction Costs**: Phase 1 homes (~40 units)
7. **Sales & Marketing**: Broker fees, marketing materials, model homes
8. **Legal & Administrative**: Entity formation, investor documentation, ongoing compliance
9. **Financing Costs**: Interest on any debt, investor returns
10. **Contingency**: Standard 10-15% of hard costs

## Investor Return Analysis

### Seed Investors ($1M total)
- Invested: $1,000,000
- Potential return (if 35% margin on Phase 1): up to $8.4M in gross profit
- Return depends on:
  - Equity percentage retained after land equity dilution
  - Distribution waterfall structure
  - Project timeline
  - Actual costs vs. projections

### Landowner ($5M equity)
- Contributed: $5,000,000 (land value)
- Receives: Equity stake in the project entity
- Return: Proportional share of project profits
- Alignment: Landowner benefits from value creation, not just land sale price
